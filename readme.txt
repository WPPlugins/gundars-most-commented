Plugin creates a widget, that shows your most commented posts.

== Description ==

Widget, that shows your most commented posts in blog sidebar. Simple backbone, easy to understand and more easy to control via admin panel in WordPress Dashboard.

== Installation ==

1.   Upload 'gundars-most-commented' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2.   Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3.   On the 'Widgets' sub-menu of 'Appearance' you will find a new widget called 'Gundars Most Commented'.
4.   Add it to your themes sidebar.
5.   On the 'Most Commented' sub-menu of 'Settings' adjust widget display settings.
